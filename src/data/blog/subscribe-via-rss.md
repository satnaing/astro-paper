Want to stay updated whenever I publish new content? Subscribe to the RSS feed!

## What is RSS?

RSS (Really Simple Syndication) is a web feed that allows you to receive updates from websites in a standardized format. Instead of visiting multiple sites to check for new content, RSS brings the content to you.

## How to Subscribe

1. **Copy the RSS feed URL**: [/rss.xml](/rss.xml)
2. **Add it to your favorite RSS reader**

### Popular RSS Readers

- **[Feedly](https://feedly.com)** - Web-based, free tier available
- **[Inoreader](https://www.inoreader.com)** - Feature-rich, great for power users
- **[NetNewsWire](https://netnewswire.com)** - Free, open-source, Mac/iOS native
- **[Reeder](https://reederapp.com)** - Beautiful design, Mac/iOS
- **[Miniflux](https://miniflux.app)** - Self-hosted, minimalist

## Why RSS?

- **No algorithm** - You see every post, in chronological order
- **No tracking** - Your reading habits stay private
- **No distractions** - Just the content, nothing else
- **You're in control** - Subscribe and unsubscribe anytime

Hit that RSS icon next to the site title and never miss a post!
