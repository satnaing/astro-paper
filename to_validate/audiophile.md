## **PSU**

Sbooster (toridal) has a smooth spectrum that falls in between. It is clear that whether you use the USB power, or the supplied switching power supply, there is absolutely no audible improvement in the output of the DAC with linear power supplies. Beetle’s analog-linear power supply (included) should be use.

[Source](https://www.audioquest.com/resource/1039/Beetle-Manual-2018.pdf).
